0NF:
Employee (#emp_no, emp_name, dept, dept_manager, proj_id, proj_start_date, location, weeks_on_project)

1NF: [multi-value, composite, repeating group]
Emp-Proj (#*emp_no, #proj_id, proj_start_date, weeks_on_project)
Proj_loc (#proj_id, location)
Employee (#emp_no, emp_name, dept, dept_manager)

2NF: partial dependency
Employee (#emp_no, emp_name, dept, dept_manager)
Proj (#proj_id, proj_start_date, location)
Emp-Proj (#*emp_no, #proj_id, weeks_on_project)

3NF: Transitive Dependency
Employee (#emp_no, emp_name, *dept)
Dept (#dept, dept_manager)
Proj (#proj_id, proj_start_date, location)
Emp-Proj (#*emp_no, #proj_id, weeks_on_project)
